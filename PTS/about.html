About us

About Our Website:

This website can be trusted in providing the top 10 list of best tourist spots in Pangasinan. We're proud to offer guidance to people whom want to see beauty of Pangasinan.

Our story:

Chivago Company was conceptualized by Phinma - University of Pangasinan, Bachelor of Science and Information Technology students, like the most start-ups, the site's first plan was vague and an almost empty paper. In this project it was decided by the project manager and the joined founding team to create a website that offers exclusive spots of Pangasinan.
Our focus was not to only provide locations but to accompany culture built on the subject places.

Chivago company:

Founded in September 19,2019 Chivago Company is a sincere company with a straight forward vision mission and goals . As we undergo the process of creating a website that can catch up the eyes of the tourists there are many struggles and problem that we, the company overcome especially when we choose the best tourist destination of Pangasinan because it is really hard to choose because Pangasinan has alot more to offer. We believe that there is a better way to promote the beautiful gem of Pangasinan and as a team we are obsessively passionate to help the tourist to decide to share memories and try out the hidden beauty of Pangasinan . With this in mind, we gather people to be part of the wonderful beginning of the success of the company . As a team we had an amazing journey for who we are today . We found solid members that are very professional and effective members of the company that are capable enough to work hard and now we are confident enough and very boastful to offer you a website that can help you to see the wonderful beauty of Pangasinan .
