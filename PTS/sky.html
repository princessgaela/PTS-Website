Sky Plaza

SPOTS REVIEW:

Built on the hillsides facing the majestic Caraballo Mountains, Sky Plaza is a public park and view deck where visitors can enjoy sweeping scenes of Natividad and its neighboring towns. It is a popular hangout spot with adequate facilities: picnic tables, pay toilets, a small sari-sari store, a chapel, and even a 120-meter zip line attraction.
