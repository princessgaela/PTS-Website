The Minor Basilica of Our Lady of the Most Holy Rosary of Manaoag

SPOTS REVIEW:

Commonly known as Manaoag Church, the Pilgrims Center of the North attracts thousands of tourists and pilgrims daily. Our Lady of Manaoag is a religious shrine with countless miraculous tales and healing powers. The devout Filipino Catholics revered Our Lady of Manaoag as the patroness of the sick, the helpless and the needy.

WHAT YO KNOW?

LOCATION:

Milo St, Manaoag,Pangasinan.
