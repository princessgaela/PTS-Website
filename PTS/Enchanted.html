Enchanted Cave

SPOTS REVIEW:

Enchanted Cave is one of the must-visit attractions in Bolinao, Pangasinan. The entire place is stunning since it’s like a huge coral reef that emerged from underwater. However, there are certain areas inside the caves that are restricted from tourists. Some people might say this place is overrated, but it's definitely something you should have on your itinerary if you are in the area.

WHAT TO KNOW?

LOCATION:

Bolinao,Pangasinan.

FEE(S):

P150 entrance fee.

OPERATION HOUR:

8 AM to 5 PM.

BEST FOR:

->Nature lover, Travel enthusiasts.

ADVENTURE LEVEL:

Wild.

HIGHLIGHT(S):

->Dip in the clean and clear water of the Enchanted Cave.

INSIDER INFO:

->It’s said that there’s a small passage to the sea at the very end of the cave and that is where the water comes from. Amazingly, the water is believed to be replaced almost every 4 seconds because of the air coming from the small passage.

SIMPLE TIPS:

->The water inside the cave is deep and it is not recommended for those who can not swim. Bringing of food and beverages except for bottled water is prohibited inside.
